Visualization Analysis

Climate change is one of the biggest threats to life on this planet. An increase in the average temperatures worldwide has resulted in more frequent and intense droughts, storms, heat waves, and rising sea levels, all of which can directly harm wildlife and wreak havoc on people's livelihoods and communities. Therefore, it has become increasingly critical to monitor such events in order to prepare and take steps to correct the current course of climate change. This visualization does a good job of showcasing the heat index over a map of the United States, mainly through its use of an appropriate color palette. The color yellow is linked to warmth, and anyone taking a look at the map can intuitively grasp that darker shades of yellow/orange depict warmer temperatures without ever taking a look at the key. Therefore, this visualization accomplishes its task in a clear manner without any scope for confusion.
