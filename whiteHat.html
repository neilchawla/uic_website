Gun Deaths in the United States

This is a much more accurate visualization as it works with a normalized deaths per population data. On hovering over the map, a tooltip provides more information about the death distribtion as well as the location of the deaths.

The underlying dataset for this visualization was transformed using the Pandas library, and then displayed on a map using D3. This visualization provides a much better idea of areas that have high gun violence, and looking at the map we can infer that the southern states are generally more prone to gun violence.
